Favourite Places

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a mausoleum located in Agra, India. It was built by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. The Taj Mahal is renowned for its stunning architecture, intricate marble work, and beautiful gardens. It is considered one of the most iconic and romantic structures in the world, attracting millions of visitors each year.

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple is a sacred shrine located in Amritsar, Punjab, India. It is the holiest site in Sikhism and is known for its stunning architecture, gilded dome, and serene atmosphere. The temple is surrounded by a beautiful pool and is a symbol of peace and unity.

Mysore Palace

The Mysore Palace is a historic palace located in Mysore, Karnataka, India. It was the residence of the Wadiyar dynasty and is known for its stunning architecture, beautiful gardens, and rich cultural heritage. The palace is a symbol of the grandeur and elegance of the erstwhile Mysore Kingdom.

Vanarasi Temple

The Vanarasi Temple is a sacred shrine located in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India. It is known for its stunning architecture, spiritual significance, and vibrant atmosphere. The temple is a symbol of devotion and is visited by thousands of pilgrims and tourists each year.
